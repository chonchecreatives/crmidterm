Art(Acrylic/Drawing/Design)

Desire

Titled "jesus are u listening"

Ever since I could hold a pencil I was always interested in art. My very first vivid memory was me winning a clown drawing contest in 2nd grade, only using 3 colors and got it hung up in the main library here in Las Vegas. My main area of "expertise" would be acrylic, as I find it way easier then oil paint and a bit cheaper lol. Most of my art stems from my trauma growing up in a religious household. If im not painting I also love drawing. Its where i get to express myself more frely as I can easily make mistakes without have to worry about colors mmxing etc. From painting and drawing is where I found interest in design as it allowed be to be more professinal while still doing art.

Makeup

Expression

Image for my photoshop class

I have always loved the concept of makeup. being able to add a few shadows or colors to your face and look different blows my mind. I started playling around with makeup around summer 2020 as thats when I finally came out and let all my bottled up emotionas come out through art. I like to play around with blood as I find its a cool concept and its a stereotype that queer people love them some horror hahaha. Makeup also me to express my emotions that I couldnt as a child and lets me create a persona online that represents what I feel inside.
